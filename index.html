North Star

Craft workflows that feel intuitive, predictable, and in control — so users can move through complex tasks without friction or second-guessing.

Objective 01

Drive consistency in interaction and behaviour.

The product should behave predictably across every workflow — what users learn once, they should be able to trust everywhere.
